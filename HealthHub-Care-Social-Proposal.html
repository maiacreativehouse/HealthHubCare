01 · Overview

Understanding Your Needs

The telehealth wellness space is more crowded than ever, and most platforms are producing the same generic, clinical content. HealthHub Care offers something more: board-certified providers, transparent one-time pricing, an in-house pharmacy, and a full range of treatments from weight loss and hormone therapy to NAD+, peptides, and anti-aging. That depth of care isn't being felt on social yet. That's the gap we close.

Your Objectives
  • Build a clear, consistent brand presence before scaling content volume
  • Post 3–4 times per week across Instagram and Facebook
  • Grow an engaged community around health, wellness, and self-care
  • Establish content pillars, visual identity, and a strategic posting cadence
  • Build trust and authority in the wellness & telehealth space
  • Convert followers into consultations through lead generation automation

A note on healthcare content. Telehealth and compounded medication content operates in a regulated space, FDA guidelines, FTC disclosure requirements, and platform-specific health content policies all apply. Every piece of content we create is designed to be both compelling and compliant. Navigating this landscape is part of the work, not an afterthought.
